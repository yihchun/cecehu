Heavenly First Birthday

Author: Evelyn

Dear Cecelia,

Happy 1 year heavenly birthday today! Your sisters and I baked a birthday cake and wrote cards for you. Wishing you were here to share the delicious sprinkles cake we made. Your sisters miss you and tried to imagine how you would have reacted to your cake--smash it? Dig your face in it? Or ask for a fork? They've also picked out a spot for your highchair at our table. We miss you, and a lot of other friends also miss you, but I know you are doing well up there. We will see you when we get there too.

Love,
Mommy (and Daddy)
