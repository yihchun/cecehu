What Ce's sisters wanted to tell her (while she was still in my belly)

Author: Evelyn

Humor heals, and kids say the funniest things. I wanted to include some of the lighter memories of Ce with her sisters. When I was almost due, I asked for the girls to write down what they wanted to say to Ce, and here is the list:

Annie: CeCe you are very special.
Annie: Coming out yet?
Annie: Hioh CeCe, I want to see you soon!
Annie: Happy new year to you, but you're still baking.
Annie: Good or bad, we still love you!
Annie: Knock, knock, anyone in there?
Annie: It's fun to be smart (quoting Brain Quest) but hard to get there.
Annie: Keep God's word in your heart all the time, and follow it too!
Annie: Know yourself and be yourself, don't just follow your friends, make your own decisions.
Annie: You're one of the best sisters and just because of something I say doesn't mean I don't love you.

Abby: We waited for a long time for you.
Abby: We wanted to name you, Addison then Acelia.
Abby: Is it toasty inside?
Abby: Hard work to make you and mommy had a C section to get you out.
Abby: We will always like and care for you.
Abby: Study well, catch up, be smart and most important of all is don't have daddy school [especially spelling/vocabulary (vocab for short)].
Abby: You are the one and only Cece, that means you are unique.
Abby: Be the wave, take control of your learning.
Abby: Be wise, make good choices.
Abby: Have good friendship.
Abby: You are a gift not a disaster.
